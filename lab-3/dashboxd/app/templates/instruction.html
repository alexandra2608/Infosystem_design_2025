Uploading instruction
  1. Register or Log In
    First, make sure you are registered and logged in to your Letterboxd account.
  2. Go to Your Account Settings
    Hover your mouse over your avatar (profile picture) with your username in the header at the top of the page.
    From the dropdown menu, click on Settings.
  3. Find the Data Export Section
    In the settings menu, you’ll see tabs like Profile, Auth, Avatar, etc.
    Click on the last tab, Data.
  4. Export Your Data
    On the Data tab, look for the button Export your data and click it.
    Wait for the export process to finish. You will receive a ZIP file containing all your Letterboxd data.
  5. Unzip the Exported File
    Locate the downloaded ZIP file on your computer.
    Right-click the ZIP file and choose Extract All (or use your preferred unzipping tool).
    Open the extracted folder.
  6. Clean Up Unnecessary Folders and Files
    Inside the extracted folder, you may see subfolders like Deleted, Likes, and Orphaned.
    Delete these folders — they are not needed for DashBoxd!
    Also, delete the files comments.csv and watchlist.csv.
  7. Check the Remaining Files
    After cleaning up, you should have exactly these five files left:
    • ratings.csv
    • watched.csv
    • diary.csv
    • profile.csv
    • reviews.csv
  8. Upload to DashBoxd
    Go to the Upload page of the DashBoxd app.
    Upload all five files (ratings.csv, watched.csv, diary.csv, profile.csv, reviews.csv) and click “Analyse”.
